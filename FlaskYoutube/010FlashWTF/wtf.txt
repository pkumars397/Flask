Flask-WTF is an extension for Flask that integrates WTForms, making it easy to create, validate, and render web forms in your Flask applications.

->Form Classes: Define forms as Python classes.

->Field Types: Supports many field types (StringField, PasswordField, etc.).

->Validation: Built-in and custom validators (e.g., DataRequired, Email).

->CSRF Protection: Automatically adds CSRF protection to your forms.

->Easy Rendering: Integrates with Jinja2 templates for easy form rendering.
